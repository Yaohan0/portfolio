## Context

This represents my current stage as a cybersecurity student, where I am focused on building deep technical knowledge and practical problem-solving skills rather than relying on shortcuts or memorising solutions. Through coursework, Capture-the-Flag (CTF) competitions, and personal projects, I continue to strengthen my ability to analyse systems, identify vulnerabilities, and implement effective security measures.

## Areas covered

* * **Ethical Hacking & Intrusion Prevention**

     – Learning penetration testing methodologies, vulnerability assessment, exploitation techniques, and defensive strategies to identify and mitigate security risks.

  * **Server Administration & Security**

     – Configuring, hardening, and managing Windows and Linux servers while implementing authentication, access control, and system security best practices.

  * **Enterprise Networking**

     – Designing and troubleshooting enterprise-scale networks, understanding routing and switching concepts, network protocols, and secure network architecture.

  * **Digital Forensics**

     – Investigating cyber incidents through evidence collection, file system analysis, memory and disk forensics, and applying forensic methodologies to support incident response and investigations.
