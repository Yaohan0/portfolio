Sometimes the hard part is not the topic itself, but knowing what to check next. I want this page to keep track of those moments.

If I get stuck on a tool, command, VM issue, website bug, or CTF step, I can document what happened and how I fixed it.

Over time, this should become a useful collection of small lessons that are easy to forget.
