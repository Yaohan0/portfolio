I wanted a place that feels more personal than a normal portfolio. Since I am still studying, it makes more sense for this site to show the process instead of pretending everything is already polished.

This archive is where I can keep notes, screenshots, labs, CTF attempts, mistakes, fixes, achievements, and random ideas that might be useful later.

The goal is simple: document more, forget less, and slowly build a record of what I am learning.
